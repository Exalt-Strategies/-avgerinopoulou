English PDF Version FR

I have the honor to present my candidacy for the post of Secretary-General of the Inter-Parliamentary Union (IPU).

I envision an IPU "fit for purpose" — a true "World Parliament" — impactful at the very heart of global governance; an IPU that empowers parliaments with a stronger collective voice to act decisively for democracy, peace, human rights, and sustainable prosperity for all.

An IPU that effectively addresses the pressing agendas of our times: the triple planetary crisis, the water–energy–food nexus, ocean health and the blue economy, the energy and digital transitions, and climate resilience.

My commitment is to serve with professionalism, integrity, and vision — ensuring that the IPU continues to be the trusted home of parliamentary dialogue and a capable actor in addressing critical global challenges.

Together, we can make the IPU "fit for purpose" — for democracy, for everyone.

Narrative CV

Academic Excellence &
Parliamentary Leadership

Dr. Dionysia-Theodora Avgerinopoulou is the Greek Prime Minister's Envoy for the Ocean and a Member of the Hellenic Parliament. She chairs the Special Parliamentary Committee on Environmental Protection and the Subcommittee of Watercourses, and serves on the Executive Committee of the Inter-Parliamentary Union (IPU).

Elected for four terms with the New Democracy Party, her parliamentary diplomacy has consistently advanced Greece's international engagement, aligning national priorities with the Paris Agreement, the 2030 Agenda, and the SDGs.

Parallel to her parliamentary career, Dr. Avgerinopoulou is a practicing attorney, founder of the European Institute of Law, Science and Technology, and lecturer in international law and governance at universities in Greece and abroad.

She has also worked at the Legal Service of the European Union and the EU Delegation at the UN, conducting research at the Yale Center for Environmental Law and Policy, NYU, and Columbia University.

She speaks Greek, English, French, German, and Spanish. She is the mother of one young boy.
